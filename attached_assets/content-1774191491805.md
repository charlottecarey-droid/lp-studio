## Frequently asked questions about Dandy.

What is Dandy?

Dandy is the digital dental lab that empowers thousands of dental practices with innovative technologies to advance patient care and revolutionize the way they scan, order, and manage lab work. We help dentists increase accuracy from scan to delivery by providing the best intraoral scanner for free, real-time chairside guidance and intuitive case management tools.

With Dandy, dentists achieve the full potential of digital dentistry.

What are the benefits of digital dentistry?

Digital dentistry offers several benefits. First, it ensures the highest quality of dental care by leveraging precise treatment planning and execution. Modern techniques and tools, such as the intraoral scanner and 3D printers, enhance efficiency and accuracy. Patients also benefit from a more comfortable experience and spend less time at the dentist. Reliable and accurate scans aid in diagnosis, and digital tools make early detection of dental issues possible.

In summary, digital dentistry provides efficiency, precision, and better patient outcomes than traditional approaches. Ditch the dental mold for digital dental impressions with Dandy.

What is a digital dental lab?


A digital dental laboratory like Dandy integrates cutting-edge technologies such as [intraoral scanners](https://www.meetdandy.com/technology/intraoral-scanner/) and CAD/CAM software into its workflow, revolutionizing traditional dental laboratory processes. By leveraging these tools, these labs optimize efficiency and precision in creating prosthetics and restorations, offering dentists a dental lab workflow to enhance patient care.

Digital dental labs redefine the traditional dental laboratory model through innovative approaches, providing practitioners with advanced resources to optimize workflows and outcomes.

What innovative workflows does Dandy offer?

Dandy is a modern dental lab exclusively designed as a digital lab. We produce innovative products ranging from removable 2-appointment dentures to fixed zirconia restorations that save chair time.

Unlock and streamline complex procedures with Dandy’s novel dental digital workflows and proprietary dental lab software. Simplify scanning with our [Chairside software](https://www.meetdandy.com/technology/chairside/), which guides you through each step. Eliminate the guesswork from dental implants with Dandy’s Digital Implant Readiness workflow, which helps you order scan bodies beforehand.

Simplify removable workflows by providing feedback on dentures, partials, wax rims, and try-ins via Dandy’s intuitive online portal. Many dental practices choose us as their preferred denture lab due to our 2-appointment denture workflow, making digital dentures a viable option for their practice.

What dental lab products does Dandy offer?

Dandy is a full-service dental lab offering a wide array of services, including [5-day zirconia crowns](https://www.meetdandy.com/labs/crown-and-bridge/zirconia/), [eMax crowns](https://www.meetdandy.com/labs/crown-and-bridge/emax-crowns/), [PFM crowns](https://www.meetdandy.com/labs/crown-and-bridge/porcelain-fused-to-metal/), metal crowns, dental bridges, eMax porcelain veneers, [dental implants](https://www.meetdandy.com/labs/implant-restorations/) and [dentures](https://www.meetdandy.com/labs/dentures/), [clear aligners](https://www.meetdandy.com/labs/clear-aligners/), clear retainers, and wire retainers (like a Hawley retainer).

Whether you order a zirconia crown, an eMax crown, a PFM crown, or a gold crown from Dandy, you can rest assured that our cutting-edge digital workflows and meticulous craftsmanship will deliver superior quality and precision tailored to your specifications.

You can also order sleep apnea devices from Dandy, such as a [mandibular advancement device](https://www.meetdandy.com/labs/sleep-apnea/), and [NTI appliances](https://www.meetdandy.com/labs/splints-and-guards/nti-splint/) to treat TMJ/TMD, such as an NTI night guard.

How does Dandy compare to other dental labs?


Dandy excels compared to other dental laboratories, like Glidewell Lab and DDS Dental Lab, through our innovative technologies that advance patient care and revolutionize the way dentists scan, order, and manage lab work.

With proven digital workflows and a focus on industry-leading turnaround times, Dandy delivers orders promptly without sacrificing quality. Our emphasis on real-time communication powered by our dental laboratory software ensures a supportive experience from scan to delivery.

We continuously innovate and integrate the latest advancements in dental technology to remain at the industry’s forefront, offering our dentists cutting-edge solutions.
